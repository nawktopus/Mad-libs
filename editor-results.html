What's A Story Game?

A Story Game is a word game where you type a story with empty spaces where a player will have

to provide words to accompany the empty spaces

throughout the story. You will be provided with words such as:

Noun - A noun is a person, place, or thing.

Verb - Verbs are action words such as jog, sing, or fly.

Adjective - An adjective is a word that describes a noun. An adjective can be used to describe nouns shape,size,color,or age along with many other descriptors.

Adverb - An adverb modifies a verb to end in -ly such as quietly, softly, or hastily.
